Brick

At Verduzco Masonry we offer various services of brick. If you are looking for a southern take on your house, brick is the best option. Brick on the face of the house will elevate the value and aesthetic of the house. We can make indoor chimneys, outdoor fireplace, walk-ways, pillars, and much more.

Stone

Verduzco Masonry is an expert when it comes to stonework. We can work and create a project inside and outside your home. Most clients prefer stone outside their home covering the face and around the house. We can also, create sharp arches and beautiful pillars that can upgrade your entrence way and backyard. Inside the house, we can cover your fireplace with natural stone and in case your outside BBQ with any stone. Any stone project you want, we can make.

Block

Most popular service is block walls or retaining walls. We are committed to making block walls that will serve our clients. Our block walls use rebar and a 60/40 fill to make sure that our walls will protect your home and family. Verduzco Masonry are professionals at building block walls and retaining walls that will last. Also, we offer block BBQs, benches, and planters. No project is to big or small for us.
